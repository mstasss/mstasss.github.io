About Me

I bring a unique background to my work in mental health. It's one shaped by years of technology, creative problem-solving, deep client relationships, and a passion for understanding what makes people tick.

I am currently pursuing my Master of Social Work at Cleveland State University, with the goal of being a licensed therapist in a few years. Through this journey, I'm integrating clinical training at the 988 Crisis Line and Adult Protective Services, with professional experience in Client relationship management using complicated technological systems. My background has taught me to listen deeply, communicate with clarity and empathy, and approach complex challenges with both creativity and rigor.

My path from technology into social work reflects my belief that meaningful work honors the whole person. Their context, their creativity, and their capacity for growth. I'm committed to bringing this holistic perspective to mental health and wellbeing.
